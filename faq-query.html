What do you need help with?

Time your Booking

How do I change or cancel a flight or hotel reservation?
Please call our office on +000 123456789 during working hours and provide us with your reservation number. Please note that some air tickets and hotel reservations are non-refundable. Please see the airline’s and/or hotel’s policy for more details. Cancellation fees may apply.
How many pieces of luggage can I take?
There are two types of luggage on a flight, cabin or hand luggage, and checked-in luggage. Most airlines will allow you multiple pieces of checked-in luggage, but will restrict it by weight and/or size. Special allowances may exist for passengers with disabilities or people travelling with infants. You must check with your airline what is allowed.
What happens if the flight is delayed and I am late arriving at the hotel?
If you are travelling independently and know you are going to be late you should contact the hotel and let them know. You need to ask them to hold the room for you. If your hotel has been booked as part of a package and includes transfer from the airport, the delay will have been communicated by the tour agency and arrangements made to ensure you are picked up and transferred and that your room is still available.
Is auto-cancellation available for all flights?
Auto-cancellation is available if the flight departure time is more than 24 hours from the time of cancellation requsted. Please check "My Trips" section after you log in to Rehlat to confirm if the feature is available for your flight booking.

Booking

Do I need to confirm my booking?
No. After you’ve booked your flight or hotel, you do not need to reconfirm it. Once you complete your booking, we will email you the details of your confirmed reservation. This email will have your flight and hotel check-in information. Your booking details can also be viewed by signing into your account and going to My Bookings.
How do I print my e-ticket?
Please login using your username and password and then click on My Booking at the top right corner of the page. If you booked as a Guest (without registering or without logging in), please call our office on +000-123456789 during business hours and give us your reservation number.
How do I get my boarding pass?
Your boarding pass will be given to you by the airline when you check-in for your flight at the airport. Please print the e-ticket that was emailed to you and bring it with you to the airport for check-in and remember to take your ID or passport with you.
What happens if I miss a flight?
When you arrive at the airport you should go to check-in. If check-in has been already closed, you can ask to be put on another flight. Depending on flight availability and the airline’s policies and the reason for your late arrival they may be able to accommodate you. However, you will probably have to pay for another flight. Depending on your reasons for missing your flight you might be able to claim the cost of your flight from your travel insurance. Please check your travel insurance for details.

Payment

How do I pay?
You can pay with KNET, CashU, Visa, or MasterCard after you choose your flight and/or hotel.
Is it safe to use my credit/debit card online?
Yes, we use a secure server for all our credit card and debit card transactions so you do not need to worry. You can print out your receipt and we will send you an email confirming your booking. If you do not receive a confirmation email ( and it is not in your spam box ) please call us on +000-123456789 during working hours.
Do the prices include tax?
The price you see at the start of your search includes all taxes and fees - except for credit card fees which will only be added when you select your payment method. If you add travel insurance to your package the insurance fee will also be added before you pay.
What sort of travel insurance do you provide?
You must select the insurance that is to be covered during the process of reservation. Please see the insurance terms and conditions for further information.

My Account

Is there any registration fee?
There's no registration fee, nor any obligation to buy.
Is my account information safe?
We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard and help prevent unauthorized access to your information and to maintain data security. We assume no liability or responsibility for disclosure of your information due to errors in transmission, unauthorized third-party access or other causes beyond our control. You play an important role in keeping your personal information secure. You should not share your user name, password, or other security information of your Yatra account with anyone. If we receive instructions using your username and password, we will consider that you have authorized the instructions.
How does it work?
You may ask us to verify whether we are processing personal data about you, and if so, to provide more specific information. These safeguards take into account the sensitivity of the information that we collect, process and store and the current state of technology. We follow generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it. The third party with respect to payment gateway and payment processing are all validated as compliant with the payment card industry standard (generally referred to as PCI compliant service providers).
What if i Forgot my password and account id?
If a user has forgotten their user ID and password, they are asked to enter their email address. An email containing their user ID is emailed to them.
